Summary: Lattitude vs. Humidity

There are two points that are interesting about this graph. First, it seems that there is little to no correlation between humidity and latitude. It seems that overall that the maximum humidity is 100%, except for one unknown station. It is unknown whether this humidity is an erorr, or an extrodinary case.

Second, it seems thatthat overall, most places seem to have over 50% humidity. I believe this is because most weather stations tend to be places near water, either near a coast, or on a river, which increases humidity drastically. This shouldn't be taken as a true represenative of Earth's humidity, only of the populated Earth's humidity.
